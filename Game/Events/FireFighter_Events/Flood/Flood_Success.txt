Hurray! The hard work of Darmsville's residents pays off as the floodwaters begin to recede. With teamwork and quick thinking, they manage to protect most of the town. The kids are playing a big part, showing courage and helping out wherever they can. As the sun comes out, everyone is relieved and thankful that the worst is over. The streets are wet but safe again, and the town is filled with a sense of unity and strength after overcoming this watery ordeal together.
