Disaster in Darmsville! A large oil spill has occurred at the town's harbor, causing trouble for the sea creatures and the environment. Dark oil is spreading in the water, and everyone is worried about the fish and birds. Children playing nearby quickly notice the spill and alert the adults. The town calls in environmental experts and volunteers to help clean up the oil and protect the marine life. The harbor, usually bustling with boats and laughter, now sees the community coming together to face this ecological challenge
