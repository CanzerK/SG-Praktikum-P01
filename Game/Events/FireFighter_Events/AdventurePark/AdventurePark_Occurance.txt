Darmsville's popular Adventure Park, known for its fun outdoor activities, faces an unexpected challenge when one of the zip-line setups malfunctions. A child is left stranded mid-zip, dangling safely in their harness but unable to move forward or backward. The park staff quickly evacuate other guests and call the fire department for a high-angle rescue. Onlookers, including several children and families, watch with concern, trusting in the fire department to safely rescue the stranded adventurer.
